# Registry Descriptions

## npm

German government cybersecurity standards MCP -- BSI IT-Grundschutz, BSI C5, BSI Technische Richtlinien, KRITIS requirements (IT-SiG 2.0), Grundschutz Bund, BfDI TOM (DSGVO Art. 32). Part of the Ansvar MCP Network (ansvar.ai/mcp).

## Smithery

Access German government cybersecurity standards including BSI IT-Grundschutz, BSI C5 (cloud), KRITIS requirements, and BfDI TOM (GDPR Art. 32). Bilingual (German + English) with FTS search, ISO 27001 cross-references, and sector-based filtering.

## Glama

German government cybersecurity standards MCP covering 6 frameworks and 167 controls. Search by keyword, sector, or ISO mapping. Bilingual German/English support.
